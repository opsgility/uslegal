## Hands-On Practice Distribution

Per client feedback, hands-on practice is woven throughout — not saved for the end:

- **Every exercise** includes guided prompting where reps write and iterate their own prompts
- **Every exercise** ends with a "Your Turn" open practice block
- **Exercise 6** provides four multi-app scenarios for extended independent practice
- Estimated total hands-on time: **~2.5 hours** of the 4-hour session

---

## HubSpot Integration

Since Copilot does not integrate directly with HubSpot and Breeze is not active, exercises include **HubSpot Bridge** callouts at natural workflow points:

| Workflow | What to Pull from HubSpot | What to Log Back |
|---|---|---|
| Before a prospect call | Deal record, contact source | Meeting prep notes from Teams Copilot |
| Before a client call | Activity history, support tickets | QBR prep notes |
| After any call | — | Meeting summary from Teams Copilot, updated deal stage, follow-up tasks |
| Expansion outreach | Current services, contract value, renewal date | New deal record, outreach logged |
| Competitive intel | Competitor fields in deal records | Updated pricing analysis |
